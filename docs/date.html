日期选择控件

v-component="date"

<div class="form-control date" placeholder="日期选择" v-component="date" v-with="date:date"></div>

使用 v-with 同步参数


Example



当前选择日期：{{scope.date1}} - {{scope.date2}}

<div class="form-control date" v-component="date" v-with="date:scope.date1"></div>
<div class="form-control date" unixtime="true" up="true" v-component="date" v-with="date:scope.date2"></div>
<br />
<p>当前选择日期：{{scope.date1}} - {{scope.date2}}</p>
